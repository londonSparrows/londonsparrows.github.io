Training

When

We train twice a week, Fridays 19:00-21:00 and Saturdays 16:00-18:00 and we welcome new players. If one of the teams has a match then training will be cancelled. Matches happen during the season (September-May), see our fixtures for more infomation. Come down to see a match if you can, its a great sport to watch

Training doesn't happen in August when we take well deserved R&R before the season starts anew Regular attendees need to pay 10 pounds a month which is outstanding value, but if you just want to try it out, it's free!

Contact Us if you'd like to know more

Where

We train at Space (here) one of the best basketball courts in London and a venue during the paraolympic games. Its very close to Hoxton Station which also happens to be wheelchair accessible for anyone coming on the East London line.
